Tacos Recipe

Description

This recipe is for the most filling tacos you have ever had. It utilises the freshest ingredients you can eat!

Ingredients

  • Taco shell (hard or soft)
  • Beef mince
  • Coriander
  • Red onion
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Cook the beef mince with taco seasoning
  2. Once cooked, put the beef mince into the taco shell
  3. Garnish with coriander, chopped red onions, and lime
