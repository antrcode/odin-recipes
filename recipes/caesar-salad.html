Caesar Salad Recipe

Description

This Caesar Salad recipe will have you in disbelief that it is so healthy! A delicious recipe for your lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Bacon
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  • Croutons
  • Chicken breast
  • lime

Steps

  1. Cut the lettuce
  2. Mix mayonnaise and dijon mustard
  3. Cut up cooked chicken breast
  4. Assemble all ingredients and add croutons
