Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This recipe is for anyone who wants to eat delicious pasta. It is an authentic recipe by an Italian grandmother.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Heat up tomato sauce
  3. Mix cooked pasta and tomato sauce together
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Garnish with basil
